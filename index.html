A Community Under Water

Chicago is know as one of the most segregated city in the U.S. and this issue has led to sprawls of communities that are sometimes solely one race or the other. It just so happens that these communities e.g Black community tend to live in areas that are highly susceptible to flooding than their counterparts. The Black Community

This map depicts the predominantly black neighborhoods in Chicago for 2014. As we can see black neighborhoods/households exist predominantly in high risk zones of flood. As seen on the map, the South Side of Chicago along with the West Side have a higher percentage of black residents, which correlates with the areas of high flood claims.

The White Community

This map depicts the white neighborhoods in Chicago for 2014. Unlike its counterpart, white neighborhoods/communities tend to exist in the North Side of Chicago as well as the East Side by Lake Michigan. Although, one may argue that Chicago is a relatively flat area as its topography will show. Flooding is not a new phenomenom in Chicago.
